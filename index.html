Privacy Terms

Privacy Policy & GDPR

UnitedVA ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency about how it is collected, processed, and shared. As a Virtual Airline within IVAO, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and IVAO's Virtual Airline privacy requirements.

1 Data Controller

The administrative team of UnitedVA is responsible for managing your personal data. For any inquiries, please contact us at contact@unitedva.pro.

2 Data Processed

We handle only the data necessary to operate the Virtual Airline:

  • Identifiers: name, internal callsign, rank.
  • Network IDs: IVAO VID.
  • Operational flight data (non-sensitive): flight plan, route, aircraft, airports, and basic telemetry exposed by public networks.
  • Contact information: details you provide via forms or communication channels.
  • Technical data: public IP address, access logs, technical cookies.

3 Purposes

  • Service provision (contract execution): roster management, online status, statistics.
  • Legitimate interest: security, abuse prevention, platform improvement.
  • Consent: optional communications and non-essential cookies.
  • To improve events and training programs.

4 Data Retention

We retain data while you remain an active member and only for the time strictly necessary for security and audit purposes. Technical logs are kept for limited periods.

5 Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Hosting and CDN providers to deliver the website.
  • Public APIs (IVAO) to display network status when relevant.

6 Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

7 Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights under GDPR:

  • Access: obtain a copy of your data.
  • Rectification: request corrections.
  • Erasure: request deletion ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction & Opposition: limit or object to processing.
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured format.
  • Withdraw consent: when processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@unitedva.pro, including your CID/VID for identification.
